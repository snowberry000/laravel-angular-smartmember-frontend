Setup Instructions

Follow these steps to install the {{integration.name}} app.

Step 1

Go to Zaxxa and create your product

Step 2

Fill in the details about your products (please consult with Zaxaa) and click on Advanced mode

Step 3

Copy the Zaxaa IPN Url below and paste into the field

Step 4

Go back to SmartMember site admin, click on Build -> Access Levels. Add new or edit your existing access level

Step 5

Go back to Zaxaa, copy your product number

Step 6

And paste into Zaxaa settings of your current access level

Step 7

Save changes to both SmartMember and Zaxaa and now your setup is completed
