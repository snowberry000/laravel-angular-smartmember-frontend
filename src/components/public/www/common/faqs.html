Frequently Asked Questions

Can I try Smart Member for free?

You can try any product or combination of products free for 14 days. You can buy, upgrade, or cancel at any time. Using the Smart Member platform to see your members with live data and event tracking is free forever.

Can I only buy one or all products?

You can start with one product or multiple products. You can add or remove products on the Smart Member platform at any time.

What's the difference between Lite and Standard?

Each product has a 'Lite' plan and a 'Standard' plan. For teams that are smaller or are new to Smart Member, the 'Lite' plan offers fewer features. You can upgrade or downgrade between a product's 'Lite' and 'Standard' plans at any time.

How do you define 'People'?

Pricing for each product is based on the number of 'People' tracked in Smart Member. There are two types of 'People' - 'Leads' and 'Members'.

A 'Lead' is a unique visitor to your website that provides you with their email address in some way - such as submitting an email optin form. You need our Smart Sites product to create optin forms and custom pages to make this easier.

A 'Member' is typically a registered user of your web or mobile app that logs in. If a 'Lead' signs up to your product (and the cookie we use to track them is present in the browser) they then become a 'Member' in Smart Member.

'Leads' and 'Members' can be imported via CSV. You can delete 'Leads' and 'Members' at any time.

How is pricing calculated when my 'People' count changes?

Your price can increase or decrease based on the total number of tracked 'People' increasing or decreasing.

Is my customer data safe with Smart Member?

Keeping our customers' data secure is the most important thing Smart Member does. We go to considerable lengths to ensure that all data sent to Smart Member is handled securely. See here for more information including our Terms of Use and Privacy Policy.

Contact us to discuss pricing. More than 4,000 businesses use Smart Member to connect with both small and large memberships. We'd love to learn about your needs and what you're interested in using Smart Member for. Let us know and we'll respond as soon as possible.
