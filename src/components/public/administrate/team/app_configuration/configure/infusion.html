Setup Instructions

Follow these steps to install the {{integration.name}} app.

Step 1

Choose which access level you want to integrate with Infusion Soft and then Copy the URL

Step 2

Go to Your E-Commerce Settings. Click on Billing Automation

Step 3

Add Trigger "After a successful purchase in the shopping cart or on an order form"

Step 4

Select the product you want to associate with the access level from SmartMember and then choose "Send an http post to another server" from the dropdown

Step 5

Paste the URL copied from step 1 and click Save
