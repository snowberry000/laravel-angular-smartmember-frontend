Setup Instructions

Follow these steps to install the {{integration.name}} app.

Step 1

Click "Connect New Account." and then log into vimeo.

Step 2

Click Allow, and you'll have your Vimeo account connected to Smart Member

Step 3

Click Content > Import. Then click the VIMEO option to find your integrated account, and SM site you want to import to

Step 4

A List of your VIMEO videos will pop up, and you will have the option to begin adding them as lessons.
